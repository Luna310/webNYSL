About As

  • Mission

    To support young athletes living in Chicago´s northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skils related to the game of soccer, specifically those skils around team cooperation and good sportsmanship

  • Vision

    The Northside Youth Soccer Leage aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athles through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

  • General Information

    The Northside Youth Soccer Leage was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago´s northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer.To be a member of NYSL,you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small ful-time staff, and relies on the generous volumteer time of parents and previous leage members.
